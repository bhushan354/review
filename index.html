Dr. Norman Borlaug

Dr. Norman Borlaug, father of the Green Revolution

Dr. Norman Borlaug was an American agronomist and humanitarian who led initiatives worldwide that contributed to the extensive increases in agricultural production termed the Green Revolution. Borlaug was awarded multiple honors for his contributions to agriculture, including the Nobel Peace Prize, the Presidential Medal of Freedom, and the Congressional Gold Medal.
